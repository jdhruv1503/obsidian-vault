### Broad subcategories

- *Closed class*: Prepositions etc. Mostly stay the same
- *Open class*: Whose entities can be expanded

### Nouns (open)

- Proper
- Common (countable or mass)

### Verb (open)

- Doing

### Adjectives (open)

- Desw

### Adverb (open)

- Modify things (often verbs)
- Directional, degree, manner, temporal
